Sentiment Score Explanation

The normal range for Multilingual Sentiment Analysis score is -3 to 3, but not limited to this range. The larger the absolute score value it is, the stronger the positive(+)/negative(-) sentiment information it includes in customer's review. For -0.5 to 0.5, the review might contains both positive and negative information or neural sentiment information.
